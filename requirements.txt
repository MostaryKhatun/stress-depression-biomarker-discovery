Software and Computational Requirements
Programming Environment

R (version 4.2.2) was used for statistical analysis and transcriptomic data processing.

R Packages

limma – used for differential gene expression (DEG) analysis.

WGCNA (version 1.72-1) – used for weighted gene co-expression network analysis.

Network Analysis and Visualization

NetworkAnalyst 3.0 – used for protein–protein interaction (PPI) network construction.

STRING database – used to retrieve protein interaction information.

Cytoscape (version 3.10.3) – used for visualization and analysis of biological networks.

CytoHubba plugin – used for hub gene identification through topological analysis.

Functional Enrichment Analysis

Gene Ontology (GO) – used for functional annotation and biological process analysis.

KEGG (Kyoto Encyclopedia of Genes and Genomes) – used for pathway enrichment analysis.

WikiPathways – used for pathway-based functional interpretation.

Regulatory Network Analysis

JASPAR database – used to identify transcription factor (TF)–gene interactions.

miRTarBase – used to obtain experimentally validated miRNA–gene interactions.

Molecular Docking and Visualization

AutoDock Vina – used for molecular docking simulations.

PyMOL (version 3.1.3.1) – used for visualization and preparation of protein–ligand structures.

Data Sources

NCBI Gene Expression Omnibus (GEO) – used to obtain gene expression datasets (GSE98793 and GSE63878).

PubChem database – used to obtain the chemical structures of candidate compounds.
